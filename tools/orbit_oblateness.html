Earth J2 Orbit Visualizer

Visualizes oblateness (J2) secular effects: Ω nodal precession and ω apsidal rotation. Geometry is plotted in ECI (inertial). Units: km, degrees, days.
Model details:
  • Uses standard first-order secular J2 rates for Ω and ω.
  • Orbit lines are instantaneous Keplerian orbits at selected epochs (Ω(t), ω(t)).
  • Good for visualizing precession; not a high-fidelity force model / resonance analysis.
