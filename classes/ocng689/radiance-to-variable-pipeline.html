Key concepts in this pipeline

Quick reference

Data levels L0 → L4

The pipeline is often summarized as a move from raw instrument records to corrected, retrieved, and mapped products. Many groups use L0/L1/L2/L3/L4 to communicate how processed a dataset is.

Dependencies prerequisites

You can’t retrieve a geophysical variable without first knowing where you measured, what units you’re in, and which unwanted signals you must remove (e.g., atmosphere).

Uncertainty propagation bias vs noise

Skipped steps often introduce systematic bias (hard to average away), while later steps often add sampling uncertainty (depends on coverage, compositing, and smoothing).
